Built for Thrill. Designed for Everyone.

Adventure Park combines climbing courses, rope bridges, and zip-line challenges in a safe, guided environment. Whether you are a first-time visitor or a seasoned thrill-seeker, our attractions are designed for all skill levels.

Why Visit Us

🧗

World-Class Courses

Over 20 unique climbing and rope courses ranging from beginner to expert, set across 40 acres of natural forest terrain.

🛡️

Safety First

All courses are inspected daily. Our certified guides provide full briefings and continuous support throughout your visit.

Family Friendly

Located 20 minutes from the city centre. Free parking on site. Bus route 47 stops at the main gate every 30 minutes.

What to Bring

Comfortable closed-toe shoes, weather-appropriate clothing, and a sense of adventure. We provide all safety equipment.

Our Location

Nestled in the stunning mountains of Smithers, British Columbia. A destination for adventure seekers from around the world.
